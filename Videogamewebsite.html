Video Games I Quite Enjoyed

Games That Helped Me Escape From My Everday Troubles (pictures=links to wiki pages)

Legend Of Zelda Majoras Mask

This is the first game I fell in love with.I never beat it though because I was like 5-7 and didnt understand the game all that well. I played it everyday all day untill my N64 broke.

Dark Cloud

This game was one of the first games I got for PS2. It was challenging and had very entertaining content. I never beat the game untill I repurchased a PS2 when I was in High School and even then I had to google the last boss.

Skyrim

Skyrim was a more recent one and it completly surprised me. I had played games similar to it and they blowed, I ended up renting it from redbox and never returned it. I played it over and over untill I hated it, gave it a break and then got hooked in again. For a game to have me interested from its release in 2011 till 2016 is impressive to me. Bethesda does amazing work, pretty glitchy games but very good story, graphics, and character developing.
